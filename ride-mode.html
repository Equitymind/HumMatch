How the driver-first flow works

This slice is intentionally optimized for simplicity. The driver starts the session, riders scan, HumMatch recommends the best Top 5 group songs, and the driver is the one who loads playback in Spotify, Apple Music, or karaoke lyrics.

1

Start the ride session

The driver opens Ride Mode and shows a large QR code so riders can join quickly from anywhere in the car.

2

Passengers hum

Each rider scans, joins the session, and hums once or uses a saved profile. Results stay simple and visible.

3

Driver picks the song

HumMatch suggests the best group songs. The driver picks the final song and hands off playback to the service already running in the car.

Why this driver-first view is the right MVP

  • The driver already controls the music in most rides
  • Large text makes the result set visible to the whole car
  • Top 5 songs reduce clutter and make group choice faster
  • Playback handoff stays simple without requiring deep Spotify or Apple Music integrations
  • Future synced passenger views can still be added later on top of the same session model
