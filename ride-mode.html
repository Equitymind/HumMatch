How Ride Mode works

1. Start the ride session

A driver or host opens Ride Mode and shows a large QR code for the car.

2. Passengers join

Each rider scans, joins on their phone, and completes a quick hum or uses a saved voice profile.

3. Get the best songs for the car

HumMatch recommends the best songs, duet pairings, harmony picks, and easy wins for the group.

Why this matters

Most apps start with the song and hope the people can keep up. HumMatch starts with the people in the car and works backward from their voices.

That means better lead picks, better duet suggestions, better harmony fits, and far fewer songs that collapse halfway through.

Why rideshare is the right wedge

  • Drivers already create repeated social music moments
  • Passengers are in one shared environment with low friction
  • A simple QR code turns the ride into a live HumMatch demo
  • The driver can become the affiliate origin for future upgrades

What happens next

Ride Mode is being introduced in stages. The first stage makes the product visible on-site and explains the concept clearly. Later stages will add session flow, passenger sequencing, results tabs, affiliate attribution, and driver dashboards.
