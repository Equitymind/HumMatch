How it works

Driver-first. Optimized for the car. Done in under 90 seconds.

1

Setup

Driver opens Ride Mode, names the session, and shows the large QR code. Passengers scan from any seat.

2

Join & hum

Each rider scans, enters a name, and hums once. Up to 5 total riders per session. Driver uses saved voice profile.

3

Driver picks

HumMatch surfaces the top 5. Lead and harmony assigned. Driver chooses the song and opens it in Spotify, Apple Music, or karaoke lyrics.

Why driver-first is the right MVP

  • The driver already controls the music — this just makes the choice smarter
  • Large text and top 5 only means everyone in the car can read the results at a glance
  • Simple playback handoff works today without any Spotify or Apple Music API partnership
  • Up to 5 total riders per session, including the driver's saved voice as participant 1
  • Synced passenger view is a natural Stage 3 addition — not a hard dependency for MVP
