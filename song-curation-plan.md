# HumMatch Song Curation - Layer 1 (Anchors)

## Target: 500-1,000 high-recognition, high-singability songs

## Sources:
1. Billboard Hot 100 all-time classics (top 500)
2. AFI's 100 Greatest Movie Songs
3. Rolling Stone 500 Greatest Songs (filtered for singability)
4. Karaoke standard databases
5. Wedding/party staples

## Vocal Range Strategy:
- Use known artist vocal ranges as baseline
- Cross-reference with existing 1,242 songs for patterns
- Estimate from genre when specific data unavailable
- Brightness based on genre/style

## Quality filters:
- High recognition (most people know the song)
- Singable (not too complex, good for karaoke)
- Vocal range data available or estimable
- Era diversity (60s-2020s)
- Genre balance

## Working in batches:
- Batch 1: Billboard classics 1960s-1970s (~100 songs)
- Batch 2: Billboard classics 1980s (~100 songs)
- Batch 3: Billboard classics 1990s (~100 songs)
- Batch 4: Billboard classics 2000s (~100 songs)
- Batch 5: Movie songs + party anthems (~100 songs)
- Batch 6-10: Fill gaps, balance voice types (~500 songs)
