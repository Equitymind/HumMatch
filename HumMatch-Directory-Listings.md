I built HumMatch because most voice apps only measure pitch range, which turns out to be only half the picture. A baritone and a bass can hit the same notes but sound nothing alike — the difference is timbre (the tonal character of your voice, captured via spectral centroid analysis).

HumMatch hums you through low/mid/high notes, classifies your voice type (Bass-Baritone through Soprano), then surfaces songs matched to both your range and your tonal fingerprint.
