How It Works

Find your perfect karaoke songs in just 10 seconds

Hum for 10 Seconds

No singing required! Just hum any tune comfortably in your natural voice. Our AI listens to analyze your unique vocal range in real-time.

2

HumMatch instantly measures your lowest and highest notes, your voice type (soprano, tenor, baritone, etc.), and your vocal brightness. All processing happens in your browser—completely private!

3

Get Personalized Matches

We compare your voice against 4,456 karaoke songs in our catalog and show you the perfect matches—songs that fit your range, suit your vibe, and make you sound amazing!

4

Save & Share

Save your profile for next time, create SquadMatch sessions with friends, and get instant recommendations whenever you need them. Your perfect karaoke moment is just one hum away!
